About the Cosmic Wizard

The Cosmic Wizard is no ordinary sorcerer. Born from starlight and ancient runes, he roams the endless void, weaving galaxies like threads of silk. His magic is not bound by time or space; it flows through the cosmos itself, shaping destinies and guarding the balance between order and chaos.

Inventory

Orb of Galaxies

The Orb of Galaxies is a radiant sphere that shimmers with the light of countless stars, each swirl within it holding entire worlds in miniature. In the hands of the Cosmic Wizard, it becomes more than a relic—it is a living fragment of the universe itself. The orb grants him the power to draw strength from distant suns, weave illusions of endless galaxies, and open rifts between realms..

Chrono Sandglass

The Chronic Sunglasses are no mere fashion choice; they are woven with stardust and tempered in the glow of a dying star. When worn, they shield the Cosmic Wizard from blinding supernovas and deceptive illusions, while unveiling constellations and hidden pathways across realms. Their dark lenses shimmer faintly with galaxies, granting not only vision beyond sight but also an aura of timeless cosmic cool.

Meteor Hammer

The Meteor Hammer is forged from a fallen star, radiating immense cosmic power. Each swing leaves a trail of stardust, striking with the weight of worlds and the brilliance of the cosmos.

Mirror of Aether

The Mirror of Aether is a mystical hand mirror with a surface of liquid light that reveals illusions and hidden truths. It can reflect spells, trap a foe's reflection, and even offer fleeting glimpses into alternate realities.
